The California palm
made for smaller spaces.

The palm tree has always represented California living: warm sunlight, open spaces, and a slower way of life.

But not every home has room for a full-size landscape palm.

Known as the Pygmy Date Palm, Dwarf Date Palm, Roebelenii Palm, or by its botanical name Phoenix roebelenii, this compact palm brings the same iconic silhouette into apartments, studios, and everyday homes.

Small by nature.

Unlike traditional landscape palms, the Pygmy Date Palm grows slowly and maintains a compact silhouette indoors.

Living With ROEBELENII

01

Light

Bright indirect sunlight keeps your palm healthy. Rotate occasionally for balanced growth.

02

Water

Water when the top layer of soil feels dry. Keep moisture balanced, never soaked.

03

Size

Keep your palm in a comfortable planter. A smaller home helps maintain its shape.

04

Shape

Trim older lower leaves to preserve its clean California palm silhouette.
